Reference
Costume: Simple cotton saree or salwar kameez
Props: Jamun Vinegar bottle, warm water glass, spoon, phone/tablet

Additional Notes:
Please make sure the dialogues are spoken with confidence and clarity. Be well versed with the scripts.


Creative Format	Voiceover	Visuals	References
HOOK 1	20 saal se Ayurveda follow karti hu — yeh ek cheez hai jo sach me kaam karti hai	Standing in kitchen, holds vinegar bottle. Direct eye contact.
Hook 2	Supplements pe hazaaron kharcha karte hain log — mujhe sirf yeh ek chamach chahiye roz	Holds spoon and bottle. Kitchen counter.
Hook 3	Health video dekh rahi thi, isme jamun vinegar ka naam aaya — tab se band nhi kiya	Watching phone, picks up vinegar bottle and speaks to camera.
Hook 4	Meri saheliyon ko lagta hai koi mehengi dawai leti hu — Rs 295 ki bottle hai	Talking head, slight smile.
DIALOGUE 1	Yeh Tribalveda ka Jamun Vinegar hai — raw hai, filtered processed wala nhi. Isme living Mother of Vinegar hai	Shows bottle close-up, points to label.
DIALOGUE 2	Roz subah ek chamach garam paani me — bas. 2 minute bhi nhi lagta	Demonstrates: pours warm water, adds one spoon vinegar, stirs. Close-up of spoon pouring.
DIALOGUE 3	Pehle chhoti chhoti takleefen hoti thi — bloating, heaviness subah ko. Ab wo sab kam hai	Talking head, reflective. Gestures naturally.
DIALOGUE 4	Jamun ko Ayurveda me hamesha se recommend kiya gaya hai diabetes aur digestion ke liye — yeh koi nayi cheez nhi hai	Talking head, confident.
DIALOGUE 5	1 litre ki bottle aati hai, mahina bhar se zyada chalti hai — online order kar leti hu	Shows bottle size. Talking head.
Dialogue 6	Taste thoda strong hai, jhooth nhi bolungi — par garam paani me dilute karke manageable hai	Takes a sip, slight face, then shrugs.
CTA	Agar aapko bhi lagta hai natural cheezein sabse achi hain — yeh ek baar try karo	Holds glass with vinegar water. Product on counter.
