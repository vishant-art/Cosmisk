Reference

Hook 3	Sitting job, stress, zyada coffee — doctor ne bola liver pe dhyan do. Tab yeh start kiya	Talking head, slightly serious. Shows bottle.

DIALOGUE 1	Yeh Tribalveda ka Jamun Vinegar hai — raw, unfiltered, Mother of Vinegar wala	Shows bottle to camera, close-up of label.
DIALOGUE 2	Roz subah ek chamach garam paani me, office jaane se pehle — 2 minute, literally	Demonstrates: heats water, adds spoon of vinegar, stirs. Quick demo. Voiceover.
DIALOGUE 3	Pehle hafte notice kiya — subah ki heaviness kam. Coffee 3 cup se 1 ho gayi	Talking head, counts on fingers.
