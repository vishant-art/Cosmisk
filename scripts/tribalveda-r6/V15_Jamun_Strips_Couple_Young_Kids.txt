Based on Concept V15: Jamun Strips — Young Couple with Kids, Healthy Chocolate Alternative
Product: Jamun Strips (80g) | Price: Rs 330
Product URL: https://tribalveda.in/products/jamun-strips-80gm

Note: Client brief says "Jamun Jam" but describes Strips as chocolate alternative. Writing for Strips.

Reference
Costume: Mother — casual kurti or lounge wear
Props: Jamun Strips pack, chocolate bar/wrapper, lunchbox
Shooting Location: Kitchen / dining table, family home feel

Additional Notes:
Please make sure the dialogues are spoken with confidence and clarity. Be well versed with the scripts.


Creative Format	Voiceover	Visuals	References
HOOK 1	Mera bachcha din me 3 baar chocolate maangta hai — mana karo toh rona, de do toh guilt	Kitchen setting. Frustrated but warm expression. Holds chocolate wrapper.
Hook 2	Chocolate hatane ki zaroorat nhi — replacement dhundhne ki zaroorat hai	Puts down chocolate, picks up Jamun Strips pack.
Hook 3	Jab beti ne khud bola "Mummy yeh wala do" — tab samjhi kaam kar gaya	Smiles, holds strips pack. Relieved expression.
Hook 4	Online search kiya "healthy snacks for kids" — yeh mila, tab se chocolate ki demand aadhi	Talking head, shows pack.
DIALOGUE 1	Yeh Tribalveda ki Jamun Strips hain — 100% real jamun, hand-processed. Na added sugar, na preservatives, na artificial flavor	Opens pack, shows strips inside. Close-up.
DIALOGUE 2	Tribal women haath se banati hain — taste khatta-meetha hai, bachche ko candy jaisa lagta hai	Takes a strip, eats it. Reaction shot.
DIALOGUE 3	Lunchbox me daal deti hu, school se aaye toh haath me pakda deti hu — koi natak nhi	Shows lunchbox, places strips inside. Voiceover.
DIALOGUE 4	Jamun me iron hota hai, hemoglobin badhta hai, immunity boost karta hai — toh guilt-free de sakti hu	Talking head, confident.
DIALOGUE 5	Aur sabse acha — mujhe khud bhi pasand hai. Bachche so jaaye toh ek mai bhi kha leti hu	Takes another strip, eats it. Slight laugh.
Dialogue 6	80 grams ka pack hai — hafte bhar chalta hai agar bachche chhupa ke na khaaye	Holds pack. Slight laugh.
CTA	Agar aap bhi apne bachchon ke liye chocolate ka healthy replacement dhundh rahe ho — yeh try karo	Holds pack. Kitchen setting. Warm close.
