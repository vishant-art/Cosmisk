Reference
Costume: Husband — t-shirt or kurta; Wife — athleisure or cotton suit
Props: Seed Powder pack, glass of lukewarm water, spoon, yoga mat
Shooting Location: Living room / balcony, morning light, yoga mat visible

Additional Notes:
Please make sure the dialogues are spoken with confidence and clarity. Be well versed with the scripts. Couple format — alternating naturally.
