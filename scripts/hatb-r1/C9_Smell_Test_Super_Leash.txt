CREATOR NOTES
MALE OR FEMALE CREATOR. The DISGUST REACTION when smelling the old leash must be REAL. Don't fake it — use a genuinely old, used leash. If it doesn't smell bad enough, dampen it first (wet nylon smells worse). The two-cloth demo is the money shot — make sure both cloths are identical white at the start, film them wiping in sequence so there's no doubt. The Biothane cloth staying clean is the proof. Don't oversell the bacteria angle — one line about moisture/mud/saliva trapped in fibers is enough. The "your hands will thank you" CTA reframes the leash as something the OWNER touches daily, not just the dog. This is a hygiene product for the human, sold through the pet.
