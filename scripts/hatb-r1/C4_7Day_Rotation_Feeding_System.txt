CREATOR NOTES
MALE OR FEMALE CREATOR. The energy here is "organized pet parent who figured something out" — not preachy, not nutritionist. The rotation concept should feel like a life hack, not a lecture. Demo both products physically — open, pour, show texture. The vet reference in D1 adds credibility but keep it casual ("my vet told me" not "veterinarians recommend"). Dog should be present throughout — this is about THEIR food, show them caring about it. The value math in D5 is important — position the two together as cheaper than premium alternatives.
