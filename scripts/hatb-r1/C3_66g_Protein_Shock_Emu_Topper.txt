PRODUCTION SCRIPT



#	Type	Voiceover / Dialogue	Visual Direction
1	Hook 1	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] Aapke dog ka food — 100 gram me sirf 10 gram protein. Yeh? 66 gram. Suno.	Holds kibble bag in one hand, Emu Topper in other. Drops kibble bag. Holds topper up.
2	Hook 2	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] Maine apne dog ke food ka protein check kiya. Jo mila — shocking tha.	Creator looking at kibble bag nutritional label, eyebrows raised. Flips to camera.
3	Hook 3	[B-ROLL — HINDI VO] 10 gram protein. Yahi dete ho apne dog ko. Dekhte hain yeh kya karta hai.	Close-up: kibble on kitchen scale. Text overlay: "10g PROTEIN". Cut to Emu Topper pieces on scale.
4	D1	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] Toh yeh jo regular kibble hai — isko pakdo. 100 gram me roughly 10 gram protein milta hai. Bas.	Places kibble on scale. Shows weight. Writes "10g" on paper/whiteboard. Camera sees both.
5	D2	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] Ab yeh dekho — Human and the Beast ka Emu Topper. 100% emu meat. 100 gram me 66 gram protein.	Opens sachet. Places Emu Topper pieces on scale. Writes "66g" next to "10g". Points at difference.
6	D3	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] 6 TIMES zyada protein. Same 100 grams. Ek me filler hai, ek me sirf meat hai — farak yahi hai.	Holds both papers/numbers to camera. Taps the "66g" side.
7	D4	[B-ROLL — HINDI VO] Single ingredient — emu meat aur organs. Koi filler nahi. Hypoallergenic hai. Air-dried hai.	Close-up of topper pieces in hand. Texture visible. Sprinkles on kibble in bowl.
8	D5	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] Mera dog ka coat 2 hafte me change hua. Energy alag level pe hai. Yeh protein ka farak hai.	Creator with dog. Dog looks healthy/active. Creator pets dog, looks at camera.
9	CTA	[TALKING HEAD — HINDI] 599 se shuru hota hai. Link bio me.	Holds sachet to camera. Dog eating from bowl in background.
