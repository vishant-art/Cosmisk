Human and the Beast | UGC Script #5 — Unboxing + First Impressions


Product: 100% EMU-Meat Topper (Regular Pack 200g, Rs 999)
Format: UGC Unboxing + Talking Head + Dog Reaction | Duration: 30 sec


Concept: Classic unboxing format — package arrives, creator opens on camera, reads labels, examines texture, dog smells it before it hits the bowl. First impressions are unfiltered. The "single ingredient" label read is the credibility moment. Dog's reaction to the smell is the payoff.


COSTUMES
  * Whatever you'd actually be wearing when a package arrives — casual home clothes
  * No costume — authenticity is the point


PROPS
  * Shipping package / courier box (sealed, ready to open)
  * EMU-Meat Topper sachet inside (200g Regular Pack ideal for unboxing)
  * Scissors or hands for opening
  * Dog bowl with regular kibble
  * Dog present — ideally reacting to smell


REFERENCES
  * Unboxing is the most-watched UGC format globally
  * "What's in the box" tension → label read → texture reveal → dog reaction arc
  * Single-ingredient messaging as the "wait, really?" moment
  * Product link: https://humanandthebeast.com/products/indias-1st-100-emu-meat-meal-topper


PRODUCTION SCRIPT



#	Type	Voiceover / Dialogue	Visual Direction
1	Hook 1	[TALKING HEAD] This just arrived and my dog is already losing it from the smell.	Creator holding sealed package. Dog jumping/sniffing at it. Creator pulls package away, laughs.

CREATOR NOTES
MALE OR FEMALE CREATOR. This is the simplest script — just be genuine. Open the package, read what's on it, let the dog react. The THREE key moments that must land: (1) Reading "100% emu meat and organs" off the label — say it with genuine surprise that there's literally ONE ingredient. (2) Opening the sachet and the dog's immediate smell reaction — don't hold the dog back, let them rush in. (3) The bowl finish — dog eating everything, creator genuinely impressed. Do NOT oversell. No claims about health benefits. Let the product speak. "I get it now" as the CTA line = authentic conversion. Film the unboxing in one continuous energy — don't restart or reshoot the opening. Imperfect is better. Sizes: 100g (Rs 599), 200g (Rs 999), 500g (Rs 1,999). Show the 200g pack for best visual impact.
