Hook 1:
(VO) I found the one gift that EVERY girl I know is obsessed with.
Creator sitting on a plush beige sofa, holding a beautifully wrapped gift box with a satin ribbon.

She smiles knowingly at the camera, slow push-in, soft sunlight coming from the side.

Hook 2: 
(Th) Imagine giving a gift so good… 

(VO) All your friends text you for the link the next day.
(TH) Talks to the camera sitting on her couch and says the hook.

Overhead shot of phone screen lighting up with multiple “Where is this from??” texts. POV shot or OTS shot.

Creator picks up the perfume box beside her with a little smirk.

Hook 3:
(VO) You know that one friend who has everything? Yeah… I found something she doesn’t
The creator walks towards her cupboard and opens the door. And when she opens it, she looks up towards the top shelf. 

Then POV shot of the perfume. Shifting the focus from the creator to the box on the shelf. 

Dialogue 1:
(VO) A luxury Arabic perfume that smells like vanilla, cashmere wood, florals, citrus, and warm oud - Zainab by Oud Arabia.

(Variation 3 will be TH)

Variation 2:
Think about vanilla, cashmere wood, soft florals, bright citrus, and warm oud — that’s the signature richness of Zainab by Oud Arabia.
B-roll: Slow unboxing on a marble table. Velvet background around it.

Close-up of fingers lifting the lid.

Bottle revealed with a soft light sweep.

Creator sitting by her vanity, lightly spraying it on her wrist.

Variation 2:
Close up shot of the product kept on a mirror, with droplets going down on the bottle. And an incense stick smoke coming from behind to add that dramatic effect.

Variation 3:
(TH) The creator talks to the camera taking the box in her hand and says the dialogue. 

B-roll of her smelling the scent of the perfume and smiling.

Dialogue 2: 
(VO) And because it’s oil-based with 48-hour longevity, 

(Th) all my friends literally can’t stop raving about it.
Selfie camera angle. Sprays the perfume and then smells it. 

And says the dialogue. Mid shot.

Dialogue 3:
(VO) The packaging feels so premium. The box, the bottle. It looks like a luxury gift without trying too hard.
Showing the whole box and the product. Tight macro shots. 

Dialogue 4:
(TH) And not just the girls -

(VO) even my boyfriend stole it once because it’s unisex and honestly Divine.
(TH) Talks to the camera, mid shot. Sitting on the couch.

A male hand entering the frame and taking the perfume from her hand. 

Dialogue 5:
(TH) Wear it once and the compliments won’t stop.
(TH) Talks to the camera with the box in her hand. Mid shot. Living room sofa.

CTA
(Vo) So gift luxury, not basics. Check out perfumes from Oud Arabia and thank me later.

SHOP NOW!
Showing the perfume in a bag , twirling it around and then leaving the frame. 

End slide.
